Welcome to OpenMedTech Lab!

OpenMedTech Lab is a collaborative community dedicated to advancing medical technology through innovative open source solutions. The convenor of the OpenMedTech Lab is Dr. Monzurul Alam. We bring together developers, engineers, healthcare professionals, and enthusiasts to create impactful projects for the community. Anyone from anywhere is welcome to contribute to our projects!

Projects

OpenXstim: Open source programmable electrical stimulator

Non-invasive Electrical Stimulation (ES) is a classic method to artificially activate the nerve or muscle fibres, especially after neurological injury or chronic conditions to engage them in activities which otherwise are not possible. The objective of this project was to develop a programmable electrical stimulator to allow researchers to develop successful stimulation therapies to restore functions in people with neurological injuries or conditions. Please visit the project repository to learn more.

Please visit the project GitHub repository to learn more.

OpenXwheel: Open source standing wheelchair

Regular standing after paralysing injury is very essential for healthy living. A number of secondary complications among the wheelchair users can be prevented just by regular standing. The objective of the project was to design a low-cost open source standing wheelchair for people with paralysis.

Please visit the project GitHub repository to learn more.

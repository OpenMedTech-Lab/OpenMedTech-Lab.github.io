Our lab focuses on developing cutting-edge technologies for medical applications. From software solutions to hardware devices, we aim to revolutionize healthcare practices and improve patient outcomes.

Projects

  • OpenXstim

    OpenXstim is a groundbreaking project that focuses on developing innovative stimulation techniques for medical applications. It aims to enhance treatment options and improve patient outcomes.

  • OpenXamp

    OpenXamp is a project dedicated to the development of advanced amplification technologies for medical devices. It seeks to improve signal processing and provide more accurate data in healthcare applications.

  • OpenXstand

    OpenXstand involves the creation of a versatile and adaptable medical device stand. This project aims to enhance the usability and flexibility of various medical equipment in clinical settings.

  • OpenXwheel

    OpenXwheel focuses on the development of a smart wheelchair system with advanced navigation and control features. It aims to improve mobility and enhance the overall quality of life for individuals with mobility challenges.

  • OpenXwalk

    OpenXwalk is a project dedicated to creating innovative walking assistance devices. It aims to provide support and stability for individuals with walking difficulties, contributing to increased mobility and independence.

Welcome to Open-Source Medical Technology Lab!

Welcome to the Open Source Medical Technology Lab, a collaborative hub where innovation meets compassion. Our lab is committed to advancing healthcare through the power of open-source solutions. By harnessing the collective knowledge and creativity of a diverse community, we strive to break down barriers, promote accessibility, and revolutionize the landscape of medical technology.

Projects

  • OpenXstim: OpenXstim is a project focused on developing open-source stimulation devices for medical applications. It aims to create versatile and customizable stimulators to support various research and therapeutic needs.
  • OpenXamp: OpenXamp is an open-source amplifier project designed to enhance signal processing in medical devices. This project strives to provide a flexible and reliable platform for amplifying physiological signals for research and clinical purposes.
  • OpenXstand: OpenXstand is dedicated to creating open-source medical equipment stands. These stands are designed to accommodate various medical devices, providing a stable and adaptable solution for laboratories and healthcare environments.
  • OpenXwheel: OpenXwheel is a project centered around the development of open-source motorized wheelchairs. By incorporating customizable features and an accessible design, OpenXwheel aims to empower users with mobility challenges and enhance their independence.
  • OpenXwalk: OpenXwalk focuses on open-source technology for gait analysis and walking assistance. This project aims to provide tools and solutions to analyze and improve walking patterns, supporting rehabilitation and research in the field of mobility.
